EDITORIAL COMMITTEE

The Editorial Committee at IJSSE is comprised of distinguished scholars and experts in the fields of social sciences and education. This esteemed group of professionals plays a pivotal role in ensuring the quality and integrity of the research published in our journal. Members of the Editorial Committee are selected for their expertise, experience, and commitment to advancing knowledge in their respective disciplines. The committee actively participates in the peer-review process, providing valuable insights and assessments to maintain the high standards of academic excellence that define IJSSE. Their dedication to fostering rigorous and fair evaluation contributes significantly to the credibility and impact of the journal.

EDITORIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Editors at IJSSE bear a crucial responsibility in overseeing the publication process with the utmost commitment to ethical and academic standards. Their role involves managing the peer-review process, ensuring that submitted manuscripts undergo thorough and unbiased evaluation by qualified experts in the field. Editors work closely with authors to uphold the integrity of their research, offering constructive feedback and guidance to enhance the clarity and scholarly merit of accepted articles. In addition, editors oversee the implementation of ethical guidelines, preventing plagiarism, and upholding the principles of transparency and fairness. Their vigilant efforts contribute to maintaining the credibility of IJSSE as a reputable platform for scholarly discourse and advancement.
