Authors Registration

List of Papers Under an Author

Authors submitting their work to IJSSE can effortlessly access a comprehensive list of all their published papers through our user-friendly platform. This feature empowers authors to maintain an organized record of their scholarly contributions, fostering transparency and facilitating the efficient management of their academic portfolio. The list provides a consolidated view of the titles, publication dates, and key details of each paper authored by the individual, offering a convenient way to showcase their research accomplishments.

Author Instructions

IJSSE provides clear and detailed author instructions to ensure a seamless and standardized submission process. Authors are strongly encouraged to meticulously review the guidelines before preparing and submitting their manuscripts. The author instructions cover essential aspects such as manuscript formatting, citation styles, and submission procedures. Adhering to these instructions enhances the efficiency of the editorial process and promotes consistency in the presentation of scholarly work. Authors can find the comprehensive set of guidelines on our website, empowering them to navigate the submission process with confidence.

Publication Charge

To support the open-access model and maintain the high quality of our publications, IJSSE levies a nominal publication charge upon acceptance of a manuscript. This charge contributes to the editorial and production processes, ensuring that the journal remains a sustainable platform for scholarly dissemination. Detailed information about publication charges, including payment methods and any available waivers or discounts, can be found in the "Publication Charge" section on our website.

Article Template

Authors submitting manuscripts to IJSSE are encouraged to use our provided article template to ensure adherence to the journal's formatting standards. The template is designed to facilitate the creation of well-organized and professionally formatted manuscripts. By using the IJSSE article template, authors can streamline the preparation of their submissions, focusing on the content and scientific merit of their research. The template is available for download on our website, accompanied by clear instructions to guide authors through the formatting process.

Copyrights Form Template

Upon acceptance of a manuscript for publication, authors are required to complete a copyrights form to formalize the transfer of copyright to IJSSE. The copyrights form template provides a standardized and straightforward format for authors to grant the necessary permissions for publication. By completing this form, authors contribute to the proper documentation and legal aspects of the publishing process, ensuring a clear understanding of copyright ownership and usage rights. The copyrights form template can be accessed on our website's "Author Resources" section.

Author Guidelines

IJSSE provides comprehensive author guidelines to assist researchers in preparing and submitting their manuscripts. These guidelines encompass various aspects, including manuscript structure, citation styles, ethical considerations, and submission procedures. Authors are strongly encouraged to review the guidelines thoroughly before submitting their work to ensure compliance with the journal's standards. The author guidelines serve as a valuable resource, offering insights into the expectations and requirements for manuscript preparation and facilitating a smooth and efficient editorial process.
