At IJSSE, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of publication ethics, recognizing the paramount importance of integrity and transparency in scholarly communication. Our commitment to ethical practices is integral to maintaining the credibility and trustworthiness of the research we publish. Authors submitting their work to IJSSE are expected to adhere to ethical guidelines, ensuring the originality and authenticity of their research. Our peer-review process is rigorous, unbiased, and conducted by experts in the respective fields, contributing to the reliability of the scholarly content we disseminate. We are dedicated to preventing plagiarism, data fabrication, and other forms of academic misconduct. Additionally, IJSSE upholds the confidentiality of the peer-review process and ensures that all stakeholders, including authors, reviewers, and editors, are treated with fairness and respect. By upholding these ethical principles, IJSSE strives to foster a scholarly environment that values integrity, transparency, and the pursuit of knowledge.

Welcome to the International Journal of Social Sciences and Education (IJSSE), a distinguished platform dedicated to advancing research and scholarly contributions in the fields of social sciences and education. IJSSE serves as a catalyst for the dissemination of high-quality, peer-reviewed research that spans the diverse spectrum of social sciences, encompassing sociology, psychology, economics, political science, and more, as well as education-related disciplines. Our commitment to academic excellence is reflected in our Scopus indexing, providing a reputable avenue for researchers, scholars, and practitioners to share their innovative insights and contribute to the global discourse. With a mission to foster intellectual growth and interdisciplinary collaboration, IJSSE is poised to make significant contributions to the academic community. Join us on this intellectual journey as we explore, discuss, and advance knowledge in the realms of social sciences and education.
